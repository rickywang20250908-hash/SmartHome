# Monitoring Service SOP (Guardian Plan)
# 家庭系统守护服务 SOP

## 1. Service Definition / 服务定位
**🇨🇳 我们负责“发现问题并通知”，不是 24 小时随叫随到。**
**🇺🇸 We are responsible for "Detecting & Notifying", not 24/7 on-site emergency call-out.**

---

## 2. Included Services / 包含内容
*   **7×24 Proactive Health Audit / 7×24 自动化健康巡检**
*   **Device Offline/Low Battery Alerts / 关键设备离线与低电量提醒**
*   **Remote Config Tuning / 远程配置微调**
*   **Annual System Optimization / 年度系统优化建议**

---

## 3. Excluded Items / 不包含内容 (边界)
*   **❌ On-site Emergencies / 紧急上门**: Will incur additional fees (另收费).
*   **❌ Hardware Installation / 新设备安装**: Charged per device (按设备计费).
*   **❌ 3rd Party Coordination / 第三方协调**: We do not manage your ISP or Water board.
*   **❌ Network Re-wiring / 网络重构**: Substantial labor costs involved.

---

## 4. Alert Escalation Protocol / 告警升级逻辑
1.  **Level 1**: App Push Notification / App 消息推送.
2.  **Level 2**: Secondary Notification Channel / 备用通道通知.
3.  **Level 3**: Log recorded + Final notification / 记录存盘 + 最终提醒.

---

## 5. The Scaling Mindset / 商业心态
> [!TIP]
> This service is designed to be a "Fixed-workload Revenue" stream. It prevents you from being overwhelmed by free support.
> 该服务旨在建立“固定工作量收益”，防止你陷入无尽的免费售后中。
