# Sales Operational Checklist - SmartLife Tech

## 1. The Success Flywheel (商业飞轮)
1.  **Website / 网站**: Build trust & highlight scenarios (信任感/场景化).
2.  **$99 Audit / 体检**: Low barrier entry (低门槛进入).
3.  **Risk Report / 报告**: Establish professional authority (专业报告/确立权威).
4.  **One-time Upgrade / 升级**: Generate high-value revenue (高额一次性收入).
5.  **Subscription / 月费**: Recurring stability (持续月费/稳定性).
6.  **Referrals / 转介绍**: Viral growth through trust (信任放大/转介绍).

---

## 2. The 3 "Don'ts" (避坑指南)
*   **Don't Talk Tech / 不要只讲技术**:
    *   *Instead*: Talk about **Results** (Peace of mind, preventing floods).
*   **Don't Compete on Price / 不要打价格战**:
    *   *Instead*: Position as **System Expert** (Insurance-like value).
*   **Don't Scale Too Fast / 不要过快扩张**:
    *   *Instead*: Focus on the **First 5 Clients** for perfect reviews (慢、稳、好评).

---

## 3. Road to the First 10 Households (前10户路径)
1.  Execute 5 Health Checks this month.
2.  Focus 100% on **Category A** (High-income, travel-focused).
3.  Implement the "Red Risk" referral trigger immediately after a fix.
